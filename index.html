The "Stress-Free" Documentation Service

We understand that paperwork is often the most daunting part of real estate. That’s why we’ve revolutionized the process to value your time and peace of mind.

  • Vetted by Experts: Every document is meticulously reviewed and legally drafted by our in-house legal team.
  • Concierge Assistance: On your scheduled day at the Tehsil or Court, our dedicated team member will meet you on-site with all documents prepared. No waiting, no confusion.
  • Phone Consultations: Our experts are available via phone to answer any technical or legal queries, saving you unnecessary trips to the office.
  • Flexible Delivery: Once finalized, documents can be collected from our Sector 10 office or delivered directly to your doorstep by our secure courier team.
  • Comfort & Convenience: If you arrive early from out of town, enjoy our comfortable lounge and ample parking at our Sector 10, Gurgaon facility.

This specialized documentation service is currently available in Gurgaon, Manesar, Farrukhnagar, Badshahpur, and Rewari.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about rent agreements & documentation.

How fast can you prepare a rent agreement? +
Typically within 24–48 hours after we receive required details and ID proofs.
Do you support registration and stamp duty guidance? +
Yes. We guide stamp duty value, appointment booking, documents, and the complete registration workflow.
Can I share documents online for verification? +
Yes — upload documents securely via our Quick Enquiry form. Our team reviews and responds promptly.
Is Aadhaar mandatory for rent agreement? +
Aadhaar is preferred for faster processing, but alternative valid government ID may also be accepted.
Do you provide agreements for commercial properties? +
Yes. We prepare rent agreements for residential, commercial, office, and shop properties.
