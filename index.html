Contact form

Primary contact form where users can send a message and attach a file.

  • Form page: `contact.html` — form UI (fields, file upload)
  • Server handler: `send_contact.php` — processes the form submission
  • Success page: `thankyou.html` — shown after successful submit
Notes: the form posts to send_contact.php with multipart/form-data (file upload enabled).
Open the form in your browser: http://localhost/Form-Hub/PHP-Forms/index.html
